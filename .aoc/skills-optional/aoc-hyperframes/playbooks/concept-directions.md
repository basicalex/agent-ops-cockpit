# Concept Directions Playbook

Use after brand strategy approval to create campaign directions that remain inside the strategy.

## Procedure

1. Read `brand-strategy.md` and root/subsystem design docs.
2. Produce 3-7 directions with distinct hooks, visual systems, risks, and why each is on-brand.
3. Score directions for brand fit, distinctiveness, production fit, and channel fit.
4. Reject directions that require weakening the brand soul.
5. Mark the approved directions that may move to GPT Image 2 prompt packs.

## Output

Update `hyperframes/docs/concept-directions.md`. Do not generate images in this step.
