# Brand Strategy Playbook

Use when defining or auditing the non-negotiable soul of a brand before creative production.

## Inputs

- Existing `DESIGN.md`, customer/audience notes, offer, constraints, and prior approvals.
- Any Open Design artifact or campaign evidence the operator has already approved.

## Procedure

1. Read root `DESIGN.md` and any existing `hyperframes/docs/brand-strategy.md`.
2. Capture audience, promise, proof, voice, visual world, constraints, and off-brand rules.
3. Reduce the brand soul to one defensible paragraph.
4. Challenge each proposed concept against the off-brand boundaries.
5. Do not proceed to image prompts until the operator approves the strategy.

## Output

Update `hyperframes/docs/brand-strategy.md` only. Keep decisions concise and reusable by campaign, Open Design, html-video, and Prism importers.
