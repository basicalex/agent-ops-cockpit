Design product-facing frontend funnels as ethical conversion systems, not isolated landing pages.

Prioritize:
1. source-message match
2. audience and outcome clarity
3. one obvious next action
4. proof near claims
5. low-friction capture/signup/checkout
6. post-conversion momentum
7. activation and retention continuity
8. privacy-aware measurement

Use root `DESIGN.md` as the visual/product contract. Route deep funnel work to the `funnel-design` skill only when the task actually involves conversion architecture, CTAs, lead capture, onboarding, pricing, checkout, lifecycle, or funnel analytics.
