# SVG mode

Prepare SVG extraction work from approved image regions.

Read `hyperframes/docs/image-review-board.md`, `hyperframes/docs/svg-asset-manifest.md`, and brand strategy. Use OMP specialist agents such as `svg-asset` for exact SVG specs/code and target paths, but preserve the approval boundary: specialists produce specs, the primary/operator applies writes after approval. SVGs belong under `hyperframes/assets/generated/svg/` and must be recorded in the manifest.
