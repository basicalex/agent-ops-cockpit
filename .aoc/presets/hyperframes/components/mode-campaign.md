# Campaign mode

Assemble approved strategy, copy, SVG assets, and campaign beats into HyperFrames/html-video production source.

Read `content-campaign-plan.md`, `svg-asset-manifest.md`, image review, and copy/CTA approvals. Use html-video for content-graph/template/studio/render flows and direct HyperFrames HTML/GSAP for custom motion. Use `aoc-hyperframes brand campaign ...` to scaffold linked compositions, then refresh boards/catalogs and preview before rendering. Do not render unless explicitly requested.
