# Concepts mode

Generate 3-7 campaign directions grounded in the approved strategy.

Read `hyperframes/docs/brand-strategy.md` first. Each direction needs a name, hook, audience, visual system, why it is on-brand, production fit, and risk. Reject ideas that require weakening off-brand boundaries. Output belongs in `hyperframes/docs/concept-directions.md`. Do not create GPT Image 2 prompts until directions are selected.
