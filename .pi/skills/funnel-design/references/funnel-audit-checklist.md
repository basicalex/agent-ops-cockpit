# Funnel Audit Checklist

## Product clarity

- Who is this for?
- What outcome does it create?
- Why now?
- What category is it?
- What makes it credible?

## Journey continuity

- Does every route have a next step?
- Are CTAs specific and destination-valid?
- Does conversion lead to a thank-you, booking, setup, onboarding, or clear next action?
- Are blog/docs/content pages connected to conversion when appropriate?

## Proof

- Are claims supported nearby?
- Are screenshots/examples/case studies real or clearly marked placeholders?
- Are testimonials/logos/metrics verifiable?

## Friction

- Are forms minimal?
- Is pricing/demo/trial/contact discoverable?
- Are auth/checkout steps justified?
- Are errors and loading states helpful?

## Measurement

- Are key funnel events tracked where analytics are allowed?
- Are UTM/referral values preserved?
- Are events free of secrets and sensitive personal data?

## Accessibility/performance

- Semantic headings and labels?
- Keyboard/focus states?
- Contrast and readable type?
- Responsive layout?
- Heavy media/embeds lazy-loaded?
