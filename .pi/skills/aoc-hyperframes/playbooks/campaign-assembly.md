# Campaign Assembly Playbook

Use after strategy, concept, image review, and SVG manifests are approved.

## Procedure

1. Read `content-campaign-plan.md`, `svg-asset-manifest.md`, and copy/CTA approvals.
2. Choose html-video for template/content-graph storyboard work; choose direct HyperFrames when custom HTML/GSAP motion is required.
3. Map campaign beats into `sequence`, `contrast`, and `dependency` edges for html-video ContentGraph.
4. Create HyperFrames campaign compositions with `aoc-hyperframes brand campaign ...` when source compositions are needed.
5. Refresh review boards/catalogs and preview before rendering.

## Output

Update `content-campaign-plan.md`, shotlists, composition catalog, and html-video manifest/export paths when produced.
